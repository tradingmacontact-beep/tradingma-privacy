Account Deletion Information

TradingMa stores minimal account information (username and password) securely on our servers for authentication purposes.

When you uninstall the TradingMa app, your authentication tokens are immediately revoked. This means you will no longer be able to access your account and will stop receiving notifications.

If you wish to permanently delete your account and all stored data from our servers, you can contact us at tradingmacontact@gmail.com. Once we receive your request, all associated data will be permanently erased.

No sensitive personal data (such as payment information or location) is collected or shared with third parties.
